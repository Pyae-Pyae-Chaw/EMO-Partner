It depends on the therapist you are matched with. Don't worry, the price here offering in our program is quite reasonable, and can be negotiate too.

We will firstly inspect your condition to choose a handful of appropriate therapists and this will directly send to your inbox along with price under each therapist. Choice is all yours!

It can be in various ways, such as via video conferencing (Zoom, Whatsapp & Skype), texting, and simply speaking over phone. If not international, we can actually appoint you with your therapist in person as well.

The therapists in EMO-Partner help you understand that you can do something to improve your current situation whether as a listener of you or as an advisor, a friend of you which leads changes that enhance both of your mental and physical health. We also have the therapists who specialize in only one issue apart from general therapists such as depression, anxiety, eating disorder, bipolar and many more.

You can frankly tell us the condition between you and your therapist if you feel like you are making no progress at all, and we will change the therapist for you. However, if you are on about you are no longer in need of therapy, contact us to freely discuss for more.

You can't go to therapy with 100% anonymity since you'll have to share personal details with your therapist. However, all therapists are legally and ethically bound to keep their sessions confidential and not share with anyone else what was talked about.
